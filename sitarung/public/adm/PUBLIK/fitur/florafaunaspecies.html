Goal: Biodiversity Action Plan (BAP) of Tuban NGRR Environmental Impact Assessment Towards NPI Goals (2021-2027)
Potential Impact: [Construction Phase] Potential loss of flora and fauna species caused by land clearing during the construction phase. Disturbance to fauna species may also occur as impact of the loss habitat.
Mitigation: [Minimize] Pre-disturbance survey needs to be conducted before land clearing activities to determine the availability of any important flora and fauna within land clearing area and the ability of any important flora and fauna species to survive due to the removal of vegetation.

Activity: Yearly flora and fauna assessments to be conducted and reported.
